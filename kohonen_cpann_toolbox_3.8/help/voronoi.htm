Drawing Voronoi regions on the PCA score space calculated on the Kohonen weights

The weights of the Kohonen layer can be analysed by means of Principal Component Analysis (PCA), in order to examine the relationship between variables and neurons in a global way and not variable by variable. When displaying the PCA scores, representing the variance in the Kohonen neurons, it is possible to draw Voronoi regions associated to each neuron and to colour them on the basis of the class weights.
